PSG give up on the idea of signing Lionel Messi

As per a report relayed by a Paris based news outlet, Le Parisien, former Ligue 1 champions Paris Saint Germain have almost certainly given up on their pursuits to sign Lionel Messi. Like with Sergio Ramos, the French club have been interested in signing him on a free deal for quite some time. However, the Argentine forward has made it clear that his objective is to stay in the Camp Nou.
